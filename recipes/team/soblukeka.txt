## Sunflower seed caramel spread
10 Portionen

### Zutaten

0.5 sonnenblumenkerne
0.1 zucker
0.05 öl
0.005 salz

### Anleitung
prepare a bowl to put the seeds. roast seeds in a pan (stir all the time!). add salz and zucker and caramelize (stir ALL THE TIME!), move them from pan to bowl immediately once they done. keep stirring until not ouch-hot. add öl and blend until smooth. add more öl if it does not go smooth.

### Material
stabmixer
