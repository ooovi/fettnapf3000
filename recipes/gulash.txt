## Gulash
10 Portionen

### Zutaten
0.6 soja chunks
0.5 rote zwiebeln
0.1 knobi
1.2 dosentomaten (gehackt)
0.2 tomatenmark
0.5 rotwein
0.1 helle sojasauce (glutenfrei)
2.5 kloßteig
0.2 öl
0.001 lorbeer
0.02 salz
0.01 pfeffer ganz
0.01 geräuchertes paprikapulver
0.01 zucker

### Anleitung

cover soja chunks with böling salz water. fry zwiebeln, whole knobi, whole pfeffer, laurel in öl. squeeze chunks dry and add to fry till golden. deglaze with soy sauce and wine. add tomato, zucker, salz and paprika to taste. simmer for as long as you can. then make potato dumplings as Anleitung say.
